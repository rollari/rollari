Our Story

The Ocean
Demands
Mastery

ROLLARI was born from a deep passion for Maldivian waters — a place where turquoise lagoons give way to the vast, untamed Indian Ocean. We created AMETHYST to offer something the Maldives had never seen: a truly premium, personalised ocean experience.

Every expedition is led by seasoned local experts who know these waters intimately. Whether you're pursuing blue marlin in the deep channels or drifting alongside whale sharks at dawn — with AMETHYST, you're not just a guest. You're part of the ocean.

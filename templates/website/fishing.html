The Maldives sits in the heart of the Indian Ocean, surrounded by some of the world's most productive fishing grounds. Deep oceanic channels run just kilometres from the atolls — bringing pelagic giants close to shore.

Our AMETHYST fleet is equipped with professional-grade tackle, live bait tanks, and the finest fighting chairs. Every trip is guided by captains who have fished these waters for decades.
